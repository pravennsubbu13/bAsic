Why Earthing


    Earthing provides protection from an electric shock.It does this by providing a path for a fault current to flow to earth,well technically,The purpose of earthing is to 
    provide a low impedance path for a fault that causes potential difference between externally accessible parts and live parts of the electrical appliances, so that 
    an appliance user is not subject to a hazardous electric shock.
